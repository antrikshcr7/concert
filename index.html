About Love-A-Fair

They say the journey is more beautiful than the destination and India’s pop sensation Darshan Raval is on a journey to spread love across the cities of India.

TribeVibe, Seventy Seven Entertainment, and E Positive Entertainment are bringing Darshan Raval’s eagerly-awaited “Love – A Fair – India Tour 22-23” and you are invited to be a part of the journey.

Kicking off with Pune on December 24th in 2022, Darshan Raval’s journey of love will further travel to Ahmedabad, Chandigarh, Indore, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, Nagpur, Surat, and Vadodara until 2023.
